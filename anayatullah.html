Director, ORIC

Dr. Anayat Ullah holds a PhD in Engineering and an MS in Electrical Communication Engineering from the University of Kassel, Germany. He also holds a Bachelor of Engineering Degree from NED University of Engineering and Technology, Karachi. He has served as the Chairman Electronic Engineering Department at BUITEMS. He also has vast experience in the Telecom Industry and has been associated with Pakistan Telecom Company Limited and Motorola Pakistan. His lab under the National Center of Robotics and Automation is focused on Evolutionary and Learning based Robotics.
